How It Works
From Idea to
Delivered Platform
1
Submit Your Idea
Fill out the contact form with as much detail as you can — what the app does, who uses it, how many users, integrations needed, any deadlines. The more context, the better the quote.
2
Discovery Meeting
For complex projects, we'll have a more in-depth discussion — via email or a call — to fully map out requirements, technical decisions, and realistic scope before anything is priced.
3
Proposal & Quote
I'll send a detailed proposal outlining the full scope, tech stack, milestones, timeline, and a firm quoted price. No surprises after this point.
4
Contract & Deposit
A comprehensive contract is drafted, signed by both parties, and the 50% deposit is paid. Work begins immediately after.
5
Build with Milestones
The project is built in phases with check-ins at each milestone. You review progress at key points and provide feedback before the next phase begins.
6
QA & Revisions
Three rounds of revisions after final delivery. I'll also run through the full app before handoff to catch any issues before you do.
7
Final Payment & Deployment
Final 50% payment is made. I assist with deployment and hand off the complete source code, credentials, and any documentation needed to operate the app.
